Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: your name and email address provided at registration.
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data: basic activity logs to help us maintain and improve the service.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to provide and improve Spendly. Specifically:

  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To display your expenses and spending summaries within the app.
  • To diagnose issues and improve the reliability of the service.

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell or share it with third parties for commercial purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. While no system is completely secure, we continuously work to safeguard your data.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load typefaces. This means your browser makes a request to Google's servers when you visit the site. No personal or financial data is shared with Google or any other third-party service. We do not use external analytics platforms or advertising networks.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, or would like to request access to or deletion of your data, please reach out via the contact information on our website.
